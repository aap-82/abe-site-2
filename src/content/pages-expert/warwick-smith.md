Warwick Smith is a Senior Consultant in Compliance and Operations at Corporate Development Resource Group, with over 27 years across the Australian vocational education and training sector. He is a former CEO of Training Aid Australia and of the Australian Institute of Compliance Professionals.

His specialist areas are construction, compliance, and operational design. At ABE Education, Warwick independently reviews course pages for factual and regulatory accuracy, confirming that every fee, figure, legislative reference, and regulator name reflects current government sources.
