Dominic Ogburn is a licensed NSW builder (Licence 369417C), a NSW Fair Trading Minister's Award recipient (2005), and a Standards Australia committee member with over 40 years on Australian construction sites, beginning at Stuart Brothers, Sydney builders since 1886. He has been cited in NSW Parliament and in six Sydney Morning Herald articles.

As CEO of ABE Education, Dominic has overseen the training of more than 31,000 students. He develops ABE's Owner Builder course content against each state regulator's education requirements.
